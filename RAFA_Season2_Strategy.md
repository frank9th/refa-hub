## 💡 CORE CONCEPT: "THE WORD LEAGUE"

> Season 2 reimagines the competition as a **team-based Bible knowledge league** — think Nigerian Idol meets Champions League, powered by scripture.

### Concept Pillars

1. **TEAM IDENTITY** — 10 teams of 10, each with a mentor (coach), team name, colour, and chant. Teams create tribal loyalty and fan followings that DRIVE voting.
2. **REALITY TV FORMAT** — Every stage is filmed, edited into highlight reels, and published. Content is the engine.
3. **THE VOTE IS THE VOICE** — Fans vote for their favourite contestant/team online. This is not secondary — it is the *fuel* of the event.
4. **JOURNEY STORYTELLING** — Each contestant is a character in a season-long story arc. Audiences follow their growth.

## 📺 REALITY TV PRODUCTION FRAMEWORK

### The 3 Pillars of Reality TV Experience

#### 1. CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT
Before Stage 1, every contestant gets:
- **Profile Video** (30–60 sec): filmed at home/church, featuring their story, why they joined, their favourite scripture
- **Team Introduction Reel**: 10-member team with mentor, filmed together
- Published to RAFA's social media page to begin building audiences

#### 2. NARRATIVE STRUCTURE
Each stage has a **theme/narrative arc**:
- **Stage 1** — *"The Proving Ground"* (Who has the Word?)
- **Stage 2** — *"The Refinement"* (Who can hold under pressure?)
- **Final Stage** — *"The Last Word"* (Who is RAFA's Champion?)

#### 3. POST-PRODUCTION CONTENT ENGINE
Every stage generates:
- **Full Event Recording** (YouTube / Facebook Live)
- **Highlight Reel** (5–10 min edited video, posted within 48 hrs)
- **Clip Packs**: Top 5 moments, bloopers, emotional moments, crowd reactions
- **Contestant Spotlights**: 1 per day between stages

### 🗳️ VOTING TIMELINE — HOW IT FLOWS THROUGH THE SEASON

> **Strategy**: Voting opens the week AFTER audition — not on audition day. This gives the audience 6 days to get to know contestants before they can vote. Cold strangers don't spend money. Warmed-up fans do.

| Period | Voting Status | Strategic Purpose |
|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Aug 1** — Audition Day | ⛔ CLOSED | Announce contestants; let the moment breathe |
| **Aug 2–6** — Profile Week | ⛔ CLOSED | Daily contestant videos build emotional investment |
| **Aug 7** | 🟢 **ROUND 1 OPENS** | Fans now know the contestants and want to back them |
| **Aug 8** | Voting active | Post live leaderboard teaser — "Who's leading?" |
| **Aug 9** — Stage 1 | Voting active; **closes midnight** | Announce live leaderboard at the event for drama |
| **Aug 10–15** | 🟢 **ROUND 2 OPENS** | Post-stage hype drives fresh vote surge |
| **Aug 16** — Stage 2 | Voting active; **closes midnight** | Second leaderboard revealed live on stage |
| **Aug 17 – Sep 4** | 🟢 **FINAL ROUND OPENS** | Long window maximises volume; parents go all-out |
| **Sep 5 midnight** | 🔴 **VOTING CLOSES** | Final count sealed before the Grand Final |
| **Sep 6** — Grand Final | Results revealed live | Fan Favourite winner announced at awards ceremony |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Urgency Tactics**: Post "LEADERBOARD CLOSES IN 24HRS" graphics before each window closes. Reveal the top 3 positions every 48 hours without showing exact counts — keeps the competition suspenseful and drives last-minute voting.

### PHASE 2: STAGE 1 — "THE PROVING GROUND" (August 9)

**Theme**: *Volume and Accuracy* — Who truly knows the Word?

**Stage Format**:
- All 10 teams compete (10 contestants each)
- Each contestant recites timed portions of scripture
  - Round A: 60 scriptures in 2 minutes
  - Round B: 30 scriptures in 1 minute
- Judges score accuracy, confidence, and delivery
- **Audience vote** (opened after stage) accounts for 20% of total score
- **Bottom 2 per team** risk elimination
- Judges give televised remarks (this is content gold)

**Day-of Production Checklist**:
- [ ] Stage design: backdrop with RAFA Season 2 branding, podium, scoreboard
- [ ] Live stream setup: 2 camera angles minimum, stable internet
- [ ] MC/Host secured and briefed
- [ ] Judges panel: 3 judges (respected pastors/ministers/scholars)
- [ ] Voting window opens LIVE during event announcement

- [ ] Opening ceremony: prayer, RAFA mission statement, Season 2 overview

### PHASE 3: STAGE 2 — "THE REFINEMENT" (August 16)

**Theme**: *Precision and Pressure* — Can you hold under scrutiny?

**Stage Format**:
- Remaining contestants (survivors from Stage 1 + top vote-getters)
- New challenge formats introduced:
  - **Cross-Examination Round**: Judges ask follow-up questions on recited passages
  - **Duet Round**: Two contestants from different teams recite alternate verses in real-time
  - **Speed Recall**: Rapid-fire scripture references — passage given, contestant names book/chapter/verse
- Vote window re-opens; cumulative votes tracked
- **Teams now competing for elimination immunity**

**Post-Stage 2 Content (Aug 16 – Sept 5)**:

### PHASE 4: THE FINAL — "THE LAST WORD" (September 6)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **This is the Flagship Event.** It must feel like a grand finale — elevated production, media presence, live audience, VIP seating, prizes on display.

**Venue Upgrade Recommendations**:
- Consider a larger auditorium or main sanctuary with proper stage lighting

**Final Stage Format**:

## 🖥️ VOTING SYSTEM SETUP

### Recommended Platforms
- **Votigo.com** — event voting, secure, paid
- **Poll.io** — simple online polling
- **Custom SMS/USSD voting** — built with local developer (most trusted for Nigerian audience)
- **WhatsApp Pay integration** — vote by sending payment with name code

### Voting Rules
- Each vote = ₦200
- Voters must provide name + phone number (for accountability)
- Votes are tied to individual contestants (not just teams)
- Leaderboard published every 48 hours
- Final vote window: Closes 2 hours before Final stage begins
- Fraud prevention: IP tracking, phone verification, max 50 votes per number per round
