Season Calendar

Full Season Timeline

Every phase of the competition from pre-season to the Grand Final, with key milestones and content windows mapped.

July 1 – July 31
Pre-Season Setup
Brand identity locked. Social media created. Sponsorship pitches sent. Application form live. Voting platform commissioned. Teaser campaign running.
August 1
Audition / Screening Day
Private internal screening. 100 contestants selected. Teams formed — 10 teams of 10, each assigned a mentor. Parents receive voting starter packs.
August 2 – 6
Profile Week (Voting CLOSED)
Contestant profile videos posted daily. Team reveals staggered. Mentor introductions. Parents added to WhatsApp groups. Audience falls in love with contestants before they can vote.
August 7
Voting Round 1 Opens
Voting officially opens. Parents mobilised via WhatsApp. First leaderboard teaser published. Hype: "Stage 1 tomorrow — who is leading?"
August 9
Stage 1 — The Proving Ground
Why it's called The Proving Ground: This is where raw talent meets reality. Contestants must prove they deserve their spot by quoting 60 scriptures in 2 minutes under the heat of the stage lights.

All 10 teams compete. Live stream active. Judges give filmed remarks. Leaderboard announced live. Voting closes midnight.
August 10 – 15
Post-Stage 1 Content + Round 2 Voting
Highlights reel published within 48 hrs. Contestant spotlights, parent reaction clips, BTS content. Voting Round 2 open. Leaderboard updates every 48 hrs.
August 16
Stage 2 — The Refinement
Why it's called The Refinement: The pressure increases to weed out the weak. Contestants face Cross-Examination (judges interrupting them) and Duet Challenges, refining their focus and precision.

Team immunity stakes. Leaderboard revealed live on stage. Voting closes midnight.
August 17 – September 5
Final Push + Championship Voting
Finalist features, "Road to the Final" series. Longest voting window. Voting closes Sep 5 midnight.
September 6
Grand Final — The Last Word
Why it's called The Last Word: This is the ultimate showdown. The final chance to speak, the final chance to win.

Championship night. Red carpet arrival. Live worship opener. 3 championship rounds. Parent Testimony segment. Full awards ceremony.

Community Engine

Parent Participation Strategy

Parents are not spectators — they are campaign managers, content contributors, and the primary vote mobilisers. Integrate them from Day 1.

💡A motivated parent with 200 WhatsApp contacts, converting at 10%, generates 20 votes x ₦200 = ₦4,000 per parent. With 100 parents: ₦400,000 from parents alone.

WhatsApp Groups Per Team

10 groups, mentor as admin, all parents added Audition Day. Voting links, schedules, content to share, team morale.

"Families Behind the Word" Series

30-sec parent video: "My child is competing because..." Family devotion photo + quote. Published daily Profile Week. Highest organic reach.

Reserved Family Corner at Events

Dedicated seating with team-colour banners. Parent Supporter Card on arrival. Photo station at branded backdrop. Stage introduction by team name.

Voting Starter Pack

Printed QR code card + voting link. WhatsApp message templates. Challenge card: "Can your family get 100 votes before Stage 1?" Top-voting parent gets a stage shoutout.

Parent Awards at Grand Final

Most Supportive Family. Faithful Presence Award (all 3 stages attended). Community Champion Award. Nominated before the Final to drive attendance.

Parent Testimony Moment

2-minute segment before Grand Final results. 2-3 parents, 30 seconds each. Prompt: "What has RAFA done for my child?" Emotional peak of the evening.

Viral Content Hooks

The 60-Second Challenge

Invite non-contestants to recite 10 scriptures in 60 seconds. Post and tag RAFA. Creates user-generated content wave.

Which Team Are You?

Personality quiz mapped to a team. Shareable graphic result. Drives team loyalty before anyone even knows a contestant.

Mentor vs. Student

Mentor challenges their contestant to a speed recall battle. Playful, humanising, highly shareable.

Scripture of the Day

Daily designed post with a competition scripture. Educates and promotes simultaneously.

In the Word Series

60-second daily devotional from a contestant. Builds their following and audience investment in their journey.

Parent Reaction Clips

Film and publish parent reactions to their child performing. Consistently the highest-reach, highest-share content type.
